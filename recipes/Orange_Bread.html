Orange Bread

A subtly sweet, delicious bread.

Ingredients

  • 3-5 Oranges
  • 12 eggs
  • 2 1/4 cups of sugar
  • 3 sticks of butter
  • 6 cups of pancake mix
  • 3 cups of flour
  • vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Grate 3 oranges crust off the orange
  2. Squeeze 3/5 Oranges of their juice
  3. Crack and seperate the Egg yolk, from the egg whites
  4. Beat egg whites until they're fluffed "El Pico"
  5. Add 2 1/4 cups of sugar to egg whites, an additional 1/2 cup can be added if wanted.
  6. Add the Egg yolks, 3 sticks of melted butter, Orange Juice, and orange zest
  7. Bake at 450 degrees, reduce to 350 degrees
