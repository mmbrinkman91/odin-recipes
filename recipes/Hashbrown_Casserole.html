Hashbrown Casserole

A savory, cheesy, dish.

Ingredients

  • 16oz Sour Cream
  • 1 can of cream of chicken soup
  • 3 cups shreaded cheese
  • half a stick of butter
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 - 3 cups of rice crispies
  • 9 x 13 pan
  • Hashbrowns

Steps

  1. Let frozen Hashbrowns thaw
  2. Mix the cheese, diced onion, sour cream.
  3. Add the Cream of chicken soup, and thawed hashbrowns spread into a 9 x 13 pan
  4. Melt butter into a medium bowl, add in enough rice Kripies to butter so that they are not all soggy.
  5. Bake at 350 degrees for one hour, and enjoy
