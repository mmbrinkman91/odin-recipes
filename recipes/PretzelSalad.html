Pretzel Salad

A delicious strawberry dish, very sweet, and good for parties.

Ingredients

  • 1 package 6oz of Strawberry Jelly
  • 1 containers 16oz of strawberries, sliced
  • 2 cups of pretzels, crushed
  • 1.5 sticks of butter, melted
  • 1 8oz packet of cream cheese
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 cups CoolWhip
  • 2 cups crushed pineapple
  • 9 x 13 pan

Steps

  1. Mix 2 cups of crushed pretzel and 1.5 sticks of melted butter, and spread into a 9x13 pan.
  2. Bake for 10 minutes at 350 degrees, after let cool on the side.
  3. Mix cream cheese, sugar, and cool whip, after mixed spread over the cooled pretzels
  4. Heat 2 cups of crushed pineapple, once warmed dissolve 6oz of Strawberry Jello into the pineapple, add in sliced strawberries
  5. Allow the strawberry mixture to thicken slightly, spread over cream cheese
  6. Refridgerate for 2-3 hours before serving.
